How To Order

Follow the steps below to complete your order:

  1. STEP 1: ADD YOUR DESIRED ITEM TO THE CART

    Choose your desired item, insert the quantity of the item and click on the "Add to Bag" button.

  2. STEP 2: CHECKOUT CART

    Click on "Continue Shopping" to add more items. To go to checkout page, click on the bag icon on top right of the screen or click on "Checkout" button in the pop-up when adding an item to the bag.

  3. STEP 3: ENTER CONTACT AND SHIPPING INFORMATION

    In the checkout page, fill in your contact information and shipping address. Before proceeding to payment, kindly verify all your information and items in the bag are correct. Fill in gift code if you have any to get more discount. Click on "Purchase Now" to proceed to payment.

  4. STEP 4: PAYMENT

    Confirm your billing and shipping address once again and then please choose your payment method and leave your comments if you have any. Finally, check the box below and click on the "Confirm Order" button.

  5. STEP 5: SUCCESSFULLY ORDERED

    You have now successfully purchase your item. Once we have received your payment, we will arange to deliver your item as soon as possible. A confirmation email will be sent after your transaction has been completed.

  6. STEP 6: DELIVERY UPDATE

    Once your items are dispacthed you will receive an email with the tracking number you can use to track your order.
